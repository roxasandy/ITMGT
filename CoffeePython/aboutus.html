Our mission is to make the world a better, happier place through our coffee.

Kai Coffee started as a coffee making hobby during the COVID-19 pandemic that eventually turned into a passion project. Our founder saw the ability of coffee to put a smile on people's faces and lighten up their days, something he adored. Thus, Kai Coffee was born, to serve coffee to world and put smiles on people's faces.
